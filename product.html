Soundproof Pipe System & by Ostendrof Kunststoffe

Incorporating advanced materials and cutting-edge technology, our sound insulation pipes are engineered to minimize noise pollution effectively, enhancing the comfort and quality of living and working spaces. Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond product performance, with stringent environmental standards guiding our manufacturing processes. The versatility of Ostendorf-Kunststoffe's shaft systems ensures seamless integration into various architectural designs, providing architects and engineers with flexibility and creative freedom. Likewise, our cable protection duct systems are designed for ease of installation and maintenance, contributing to streamlined operations and reduced lifecycle costs for our customers.

Powerful, innovative systems for the economical production of plastic corrugated pipes and metal pipes are complemented by our consulting and services for business development, organization of production, and optimization of production processes. We offer service and support to ensure long equipment life and increase the profitability of your business, along with innovative tube profile design tailored to customer requirements and the needs of respective markets.

Plastic RUbber Sealing System & by MDS Mayer GmbH

The Plastic Rubber Sealing System pipes represent a cutting-edge solution designed to provide reliable sealing across diverse applications. These pipes integrate advanced plastic and rubber materials, combining flexibility with robust durability to meet stringent operational demands. Engineered for superior sealing performance, they effectively prevent leaks under varying pressures and environmental conditions. Their durability ensures long-term reliability, withstanding wear and maintaining integrity over extended use. Versatile by nature, Plastic Rubber Sealing System pipes find application in plumbing, irrigation, and industrial fluid transport. They offer ease of installation and adaptability to different installation environments, thanks to the flexible rubber component supported by the structural stability of plastic.

Specialized Plastic Packaging by & Buecherhoff Pakaging Solutions

Our conductive plastic packaging with the patented Büscherhoff grid film is specially developed for use in potentially explosive environments. Büscherhoff inliners and bag-in-box systems offer individually adapted packaging solutions for liquids, available in various shapes and sizes for transport. We also provide a range of other products, including block bags, FFS films, PE, PP or conductive mesh film, and injection-molded items custom-made to customer specifications.
